Rubik Periodic Table — 54 Control Elements

The complete classification of pharmaceutical market control elements
The Atomic Structure of Market Control
54 Elements · 7 Blocks · 6 Rows
Like the chemical periodic table, Rubikalogics 54 organizes market control elements by their function, interaction potential, and position in the value chain. Each element has a specific role, valence, and predictable behavior.
🔵 Human Force (s-Block)
Field force, sales reps, KAMs — the most reactive elements that directly touch the market.
🔴 Demand Engine (p-Block)
RX, OTC, digital commerce, social media — where demand is generated.
🟣 Influence System (p-Block)
Physicians, pharmacists, KOLs, content — how decisions are shaped.
🟢 Commercial Flow (p-Block)
B2B, distribution, pricing, margin — how value moves through the market.
🔷 Digital Operations (d-Block)
CRM, B2B, PSP, CME, analytics — multi-valence transition elements.
🟠 Governance & KPI (d-Block)
Strategic decomposition, KPI engineering, incentives — the noble gases of market control.
🟡 Product Intelligence (p-Block)
Lifecycle management, portfolio balancing, RWE — closing the feedback loop.
⚠️ f-Block (Distortion)
Arbitrage, price hacking, hidden discounts, misdiagnosis — market entropy elements.
