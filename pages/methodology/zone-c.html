DEFINITION

What is Zone C?

Zone C occupies rows 7 and 8 at the bottom of the periodic table. Here, digitalization is absent. The d‑block is completely missing. A void fills the center. f‑block distortion elements become active. Inputs (strategy, resources) collapse, outputs (data, recipients) become corrupted or falsified.

Location in the Periodic Table

Zone C spans Rows 7 and 8 — the bottom of the table. Row 7 (Collapse) has a void in the center. Row 8 (Swamp) has f‑block elements filling that void. Above it lies Zone B. Nothing exists below — this is the bottom.

⚠️ A Warning

Zone C is not "random chaos". It is an alternative system with its own logic, its own heroes, and its own stability. The rep who burns the most discounts is the highest paid. Fake data becomes the "truth" on which decisions are built. This is not failure — this is a successful alternative system.
