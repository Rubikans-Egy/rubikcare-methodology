CUMULATIVE METRICS

How KPIs Accumulate — From Element to Ecosystem

🔷 Level 1: Element KPIs
Every d‑Block element produces its own report:
  • CRM → "Accepted visit rate"
  • B2B → "Digital order penetration"
  • eRX → "e‑prescription adoption"
  • PSP → "Adherence score"
🔄 Level 2: Pathway KPIs
Element indicators aggregate into pathway health scores:
  • PSP Health = Enrollment + Dispensing + Adherence + RWE
  • OTC Health = B2B + Cash Back + Forecast accuracy
🏢 Level 3: System KPIs
All pathway scores combine into enterprise‑wide metrics:
  • Digital Maturity Index (0-100)
  • Overall Compliance Score
  • Market Intelligence Quotient
