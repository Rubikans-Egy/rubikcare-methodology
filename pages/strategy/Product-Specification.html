RELEASE 1.0 · JUNE 2026

RubikCare v1.0
Patient Support Programs

Empowering pharmaceutical companies to manage digital Patient Support Programs — connecting doctors, pharmacists, and patients in a fully integrated ecosystem.

🎯 VISION

From Prescription to Persistence

RubikCare v1.0 is a bridge connecting pharmaceutical companies with physicians, pharmacists, and patients — delivering subsidized medications to eligible patients through a fully digital, compliant platform.

👥 STAKEHOLDERS

Four User Types, One Unified Platform

🏢
Pharma Companies
  • Create & manage PSP programs
  • Invite doctors & pharmacies
  • Real-time dashboards & reports
  • Budget tracking & ROI analytics
  • Sales rep mobile app
👨‍⚕️
Doctors
  • Find & join PSP programs
  • Enroll eligible patients
  • Track patient adherence
  • Upload test results & follow-ups
  • Receive CME points
💊
Pharmacists
  • Accept PSP invitations
  • Verify OTP tokens for dispensing
  • Request medication from pharma
  • Submit reimbursement claims
  • Track dispensing history
👤
Patients
  • Receive invitations from doctors
  • View active PSP programs
  • Generate OTP for medication pickup
  • Health record management
  • Medication reminders
⚙️ PLATFORM FEATURES

Complete PSP Management Suite

📋
Program Management
Create PSP programs with support type, budget, duration, and target specialties. Full lifecycle management.
📧
Invitation System
Invite doctors and pharmacies via email or QR code. Track acceptance rates.
🔐
OTP Token System
Secure one-time passwords for medication dispensing. Time-limited, traceable.
📊
Real-Time Dashboards
Live KPIs: enrolled patients, budget usage, adherence rates, geographic distribution.
📈
Advanced Reporting
Exportable reports: financial, geographic, medication, doctor performance, pharmacy performance.
🔔
Push Notifications
Real-time alerts for new invitations, patient enrollments, and dispensing activities.
