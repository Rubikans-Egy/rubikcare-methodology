Phase 4 — Data Monetization & Advertising Network
Turning anonymized health data into actionable intelligence and targeted marketing
📈 Real-World Evidence (RWE)
Anonymized patient adherence data, treatment outcomes, and clinical effectiveness — sold as annual reports to pharma companies for label expansion, HEOR studies, and regulatory submissions.
📊 Market Intelligence
Prescription patterns, physician behavior, geographic demand density — dashboards and reports for pharma market access and commercial teams.
📢 Targeted Advertising (CPM/CPC)
Pharma companies advertise CME courses to specialists. Hospitals promote services to patients in specific areas. Insurers target chronic patients.
🎯 Cross-Side Marketing
Pharma → Doctors, Hospitals → Patients, Education → Doctors, Insurance → Patients. Each stakeholder becomes both audience and advertiser.
💰 Revenue Streams — Phase 4
  • RWE Reports ($50K-200K annually per pharma)
  • Market Intelligence Dashboards ($20K-100K annually)
  • Ad Network CPM ($5-15 per 1,000 impressions)
  • Ad Network CPC ($0.50-2 per click)
  • Sponsored Content ($5K-50K per campaign)
✈️🏥
Phase 5 — RubikCare International (Medical Tourism)
Connecting international patients with Egypt's world-class healthcare providers
🌍 Why Egypt?
30-50% lower costs than GCC/Europe. World-class physicians trained internationally. Unique tourism assets (Nile, Red Sea, historical sites). Strategic location between Africa, Europe, and the Middle East.
🏥 Treatment Marketplace
Curated packages for surgery, dental, IVF, oncology, orthopedics, and wellness retreats. Vetted hospitals and clinics with quality certifications.
🎟️ Integrated Booking
Visa facilitation, flight booking, hotel accommodation, airport transfers, and post-treatment follow-up — all through the RubikCare platform.
🔗 Strategic Partnerships
Airlines (EgyptAir, Emirates, Qatar Airways), hotel chains (Marriott, Hilton, local resorts), travel agencies, and tourism boards.
💰 Revenue Streams — Phase 5
  • Hospital/Clinic Commission (5-10%)
  • Hotel Commission (5-10%)
  • Airline Commission (3-5%)
  • Package Coordination Fees
  • Visa Service Fees
🔗👥
Phase 6 — Professional Network (Healthcare LinkedIn)
The largest professional network for healthcare professionals in the MENA region
👨‍⚕️ Professional Profiles
Doctors, pharmacists, nurses, technicians, medical reps, hospital administrators — each with detailed profiles, credentials, publications, and experience.
📋 Job Board
Pharmaceutical companies, hospitals, and clinics post positions. Candidates apply directly through the platform. Recruitment commission model.
🏢 Company Pages
Pharma companies, hospitals, clinics, and medical suppliers can create branded pages, post updates, and recruit talent.
🎓 Certifications & Badges
Rubik Academy issues verified certificates and digital badges for completed courses, CME credits, and professional development.
💰 Revenue Streams — Phase 6
  • Job Posting Fees ($50-200 per post)
  • Recruitment Commission (10-15% of first-year salary)
  • Premium Profiles ($5-10/month)
  • Certificate Issuance Fees ($10-50 per certificate)
  • Company Page Subscriptions ($100-500/month)
  • Recruitment Agency Partnerships
🔓🌐
Phase 7 — API Marketplace & Platform-as-a-Service
Opening the ecosystem for third-party developers and regional expansion
🔌 Open API
Third-party developers build applications on top of RubikCare's data and services — appointment booking widgets, analytics tools, integration with hospital systems.
🏪 API Marketplace
Developers sell their applications to RubikCare users. The platform takes a commission on every transaction.
🏷️ White-Label Solutions
Pharma companies, hospital groups, and regional partners can license the RubikCare platform under their own brand.
🌍 Regional Expansion
Licensing the platform to partners in Saudi Arabia, UAE, Kuwait, Jordan, and North Africa — each paying annual fees and royalties.
💰 Revenue Streams — Phase 7
  • API Access Fees (per call / monthly subscription)
  • API Marketplace Commission (15-30%)
  • White-Label Licensing Fees ($50K-200K annually per partner)
  • Regional Franchise Fees ($100K-500K upfront + royalties)
  • Enterprise Data Licenses
