Executive Summary

The Healthcare Operating System
the Middle East Doesn't Have Yet

RubikCare is being built as a Digital Health Super Platform — not another booking app or pharmacy delivery service, but a comprehensive system that manages the complete health journey of individuals and families. Just as a Rubik's Cube solves apparent complexity through elegant, layered logic, RubikCare transforms fragmented healthcare interactions into a single organised ecosystem.

The go-to-market strategy leverages an already-existing, fully-operational network of pharmaceutical representatives, physicians, and pharmacies — turning distribution cost into a competitive advantage. Every new stakeholder added becomes a channel to acquire the next.
