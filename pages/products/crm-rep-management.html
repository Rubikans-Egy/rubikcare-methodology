⚠️ THE PROBLEM

Traditional Pharma Sales Management Is Broken

Pharma companies invest millions in sales forces but lack tools to measure true rep effectiveness. The result: wasted visits, poor physician relationships, and missed opportunities.
📊
No Quality Measurement
Current systems track visits count, not visit quality. A rep can meet 10 doctors daily but add zero value.
⏰
Disrupted Physician Time
Reps visit during busy clinic hours. Doctors are interrupted, reducing satisfaction and engagement.
📈
No ROI Visibility
Pharma companies can't measure which rep, which visit, or which approach generates actual prescriptions.
🔄
One-Way Evaluation
Reps evaluated only by managers. No feedback from physicians — the actual customers.
🏆 THE SOLUTION

Double Score Matrix — The World's First Dual-Evaluation Rep CRM

RubikCare introduces a revolutionary approach: reps evaluated by both their company AND the physicians they visit. Finally, quality matters as much as quantity.
🏢
Company Score
50%
KPIs set by pharma management:
• Visit targets achieved
• PSP enrollment numbers
• Prescription lift from territory
• Compliance with call plans
👨‍⚕️
Physician Score
50%
Ratings from doctors:
• Punctuality & professionalism
• Value of information shared
• Respect for time
• Relevance of content
💡 Why This Matters

A rep with 100 visits but low physician ratings gets a lower overall score than a rep with 60 visits but excellent ratings. This incentivizes quality over quantity — transforming pharma-physician relationships.
